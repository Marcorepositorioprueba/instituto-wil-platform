English A1: Basic Syllabus (8 Sessions)

  • Sessions 1-2: Introduction, Greetings, Alphabet, Basic Verbs (to be), Personal Pronouns.
  • Sessions 3-4: Nouns (singular/plural), Articles (a/an), Simple Sentences, Numbers 1-10.
  • Sessions 5-6: Present Simple (affirmative, negative, questions), Daily Routines, Telling Time.
  • Sessions 7-8: Describing People and Objects, Prepositions of Place (in, on, under), Final Review & Simple Conversation.

What to expect upon completion:

  • Introduce yourself and others.
  • Understand and use basic greetings and farewells.
  • Form simple sentences using the verb 'to be' and personal pronouns.
  • Ask and answer basic questions in the Present Simple tense.
  • Describe daily routines and common objects.
  • Understand and use numbers 1-10 and tell time.
  • Engage in very simple conversations about familiar topics.

German A1: Basic Syllabus

  • Weeks 1-2: Introduction, Alphabet, Greetings, Verbs 'sein'/'haben', Pronouns.
  • Weeks 3-4: Nouns (gender/plural), Articles (der/die/das), Simple Sentences.
  • Weeks 5-6: Present Tense, W-Questions & Yes/No Questions, Numbers & Time.
  • Weeks 7-8: Prepositions of Place, Describing People, Mid-term review.
  • Weeks 9-10: Modal Verbs (können), Likes/Dislikes, Food & Drink vocabulary.
  • Weeks 11-12: Final Project: "About Me" presentation. Final Review.
