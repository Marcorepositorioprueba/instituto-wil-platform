Academic Framework Justification

This diagnostic tool is designed under the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR). At the Wilhelm Wundt School of Languages, we utilize a Cognitive Immersion Strategy. This exam does not merely measure grammatical recall; it evaluates the candidate's ability to process and produce language in situational contexts. The progression from Phase 1 (A1) to Phase 4 (B2) allows for a precise mapping of the student's current interlanguage and identifies specific areas for reinforcement.
