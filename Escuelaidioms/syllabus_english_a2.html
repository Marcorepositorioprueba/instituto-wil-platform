Mastery Program: English

CEFR LEVEL A2 (Waystage)
Program Overview

El nivel A2 consolida las bases del nivel inicial y permite al estudiante comunicarse en tareas simples y cotidianas que requieren un intercambio de información sencillo y directo sobre asuntos conocidos. Es el puente hacia la independencia lingüística.

Grammar & Structures

  • Past Simple: Regular and Irregular Verbs.
  • Past Continuous: Background actions.
  • Present Perfect: Life experiences (Ever/Never).
  • Comparatives and Superlatives.
  • Future: Going to vs. Will for plans/predictions.
  • First Conditional: Real possibilities.
  • Modal Verbs: Should, Must, Have to.

Lexical Competence

  • Travel, Tourism, and Transportation.
  • Health, Fitness, and Medical Problems.
  • Shopping, Clothes, and Fashion Trends.
  • Modern Communication and Social Media.
  • Geography and Environment.
  • Hobbies, Leisure, and Entertainment.
  • Education and Career Paths.

Functional Language

  • Describing past events and stories.
  • Making comparisons between products/places.
  • Giving advice and suggestions.
  • Expressing opinions and agreement/disagreement.
  • Making predictions about the future.
  • Handling situations at a clinic or pharmacy.

Project-Based Learning

  • Module 1: "The Travel Vlog" - Present a dream destination.
  • Module 2: "Success Stories" - Biography of an inspiring figure.
  • Module 3: "Green Future" - Proposal for environmental care.

Pedagogical Justification

En el nivel A2, implementamos el Scaffolded Learning. Aumentamos la complejidad gramatical mientras mantenemos un contexto relevante para el mundo real. Fomentamos la "Fluidez sobre la Perfección" en las etapas iniciales de cada lección para construir confianza, utilizando la corrección técnica en fases de producción controlada.
