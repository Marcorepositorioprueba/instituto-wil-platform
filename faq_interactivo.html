Frequently Asked Questions

Here you will find answers to the most common questions about the Institute and school community.

What is the main goal of the Parent Association? +

The main goal is to foster a close and constructive collaboration between parents, teaching staff, and school administration to promote the integral development of students and improve school facilities and resources.

How can I participate in school activities? +

Your participation is very valuable! You can join maintenance workdays, attend general meetings, or collaborate in organizing events. Stay tuned to our event calendar and communications.

Where can I check the school event calendar? +

You can check the full calendar of events, meetings, and reports in our "Academic Calendar" section, which will be available soon.

Are there support resources for parents and students? +

Yes, we have compiled a series of tools and resources to support both parents and students. You can explore them in our Teacher Resources section, where you will find everything from digital guides to interactive tools.
